Potato Omelette

This is our first recipe

List of ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Peel, chop, salt and fry the potatoes in olive oil in a pan.
  2. Beat the eggs.
  3. Remove the oil, add the eggs and mix it with the potatoes in the pan.
  4. Cook it to your taste and enjoy!
