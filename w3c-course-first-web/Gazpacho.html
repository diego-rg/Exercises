Gazpacho

This is our third recipe

List of ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Green peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar

Steps:

  1. Wash, peel and chop all the vegetables.
  2. Mix it with salt, olve oil and vinegar in a bowl.
  3. Whisk all ingredients with an electric mixer.
  4. Refrigerate it for 4 hours.
  5. Serve it cold: enjoy!
