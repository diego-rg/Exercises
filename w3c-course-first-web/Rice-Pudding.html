Rice Pudding

This is our fourth recipe

List of ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Rice
  • Water
  • Lemon
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Heat up the milk with some cinnamon and a chop or lemon.
  2. Cook the rice in a pot.
  3. When the rice is done, mix it with the hot milk and remove.
  4. When the mix becomes thick, add sugar and cinnamon to taste
  5. Enjoy!
