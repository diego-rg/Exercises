Galician Crepes

This is our second recipe

List of ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs, the milk and the flour in a bowl.
  2. Add salt and wait 1 hour.
  3. Add a bit of olive oil in a pan.
  4. When the pan is hot, add the mixture.
  5. Fry it to your taste and enjoy!
